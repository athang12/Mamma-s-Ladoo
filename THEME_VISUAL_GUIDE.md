## 🔍 How to Test Each Theme

### Step 1: Open the Site
Visit: http://localhost:3000

### Step 2: Switch Themes
1. Click the **palette icon 🎨** in the top right header
2. Select each theme one by one

### Step 3: Watch for Changes
When you click a theme, you should see:

#### Immediate Changes:
- ⚡ **Hero images change** (2 images on the right side)
- 🎨 **Hero title changes** ("Anime Vibes", "Motivation Vibes", etc.)
- 🔴 **Background gradient shifts** (pink, beige, white, or blush)
- 🎯 **Emoji badges appear** in corners of cards
- 📝 **Section headers get emojis** (Categories, Testimonials)
- 🌈 **Color scheme shifts** (buttons, borders, text)

#### Subtle Changes:
- 🔤 **Font changes** (Bangers for Anime, Dancing Script for Valentine, etc.)
- 📦 **Card borders** (Anime gets pink borders, others don't)
- 💫 **Shadow styles** (hard for Anime, soft for Aesthetic/Valentine)
- 🎪 **Background patterns** (dots, waves, hearts, gradients)
- 🔆 **Floating blur orbs** change colors

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "I don't see changes!"
1. Hard refresh: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)
2. Clear cache and reload
3. Check console for errors (F12)

### "Images don't change"
- Images are loaded from Unsplash
- May take a moment to load
- Check internet connection

### "Emojis not showing"
- Some systems may not support all emojis
- Try different browser (Chrome recommended)

### "Theme doesn't persist"
- Check localStorage is enabled
- Check if in private/incognito mode
